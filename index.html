Twilight Sparkle

(info)

Twilight Sparkle is the central protagonist of My Little Pony: Friendship is Magic, a studious, lavender-coated unicorn who evolves into an Alicorn Princess of Friendship and eventual ruler of Equestria. Created by Lauren Faust, she represents the Element of Magic, possesses immense magical ability (teleportation, force-fields), and is voiced by Tara Strong.

Before becoming a princess, Twilight regularly sends friendship reports to Princess Celestia. In Owl's Well That Ends Well, she adopts a pet owl named Owlowiscious. As of The Last Problem, she is the ruler of Equestria.
